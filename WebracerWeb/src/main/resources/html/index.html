Welcome to Webracer

The rules:
Each car can turn left, go straight on, or turn right.
Each car can drive at 3 speeds: 0, 1 and 2. On grass speed 2 is not possible.
Hitting the wall ends the race for you.
The game is kind of round based: each round is 1 second, and in each round you can only change steering OR speed in 1 step.
Control your car with the keyboard arrow keys: up = accelerate, down = brake, left & right = turn the steering wheel.
The blue box displays the current steering, the current speed, and your command for the next round.
The red box shows messages from the race control.
The race starts when at least 2 players joined and no one joined for 10 seconds.
